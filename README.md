# 🛒 Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

##  Project Overview
An end-to-end business intelligence project analyzing 100,000+ real orders from Olist — Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplace.
Built entirely in Power BI with multi-table data modeling, Power Query cleaning, and DAX measures.

##  Business Questions Answered
- Which product categories drive the most revenue?
- Are orders being delivered on time?
- Which states have the highest customer satisfaction?
- What payment methods do customers prefer?
- Which sellers generate the most revenue?

##  Dashboard Pages
Executive Overview - Revenue, Orders, KPIs, Trends 
Delivery & Operations - Delivery performance, Late orders, State analysis 
Customer & Seller Intel - Satisfaction, Seller revenue, Payment behaviour 

##  Key Insights
- São Paulo drives 42% of total platform revenue
- 89.8% of orders delivered on time
- 5-star rated customers receive orders 4 days faster than 1-star customers
- Credit card is the dominant payment method (73% of revenue)
- Bed Bath Table is the top category by order volume

##  Tools & Techniques Used
- **Power BI Desktop** — Dashboard design & DAX measures
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning & transformation
- **DAX** — 12 custom measures including MoM%, On-Time Rate, CLV
- **Data Modeling** — Star schema with 7 relationships across 9 tables

##  Dataset
- **Source:** Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset
- **Platform:** Kaggle
- **Size:** 100K+ orders | 9 CSV files | 2016–2018
- **Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce
